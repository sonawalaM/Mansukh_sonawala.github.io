About Me

Engineering data systems
that are built to last

I'm a Data Engineer based in Bengaluru with a track record of building production-grade data infrastructure across four companies — from early-stage startups to global e-commerce platforms. My work spans real-time streaming, financial data systems, cloud architecture, and increasingly AI-assisted engineering.

My technical range covers the full pipeline lifecycle: real-time ingestion with Kafka and SQS, large-scale processing with Databricks, Spark, and PySpark, transformation with dbt and SQL, cloud infrastructure with Terraform and AWS, and analytics delivery with Tableau and Looker. I've operated these systems in production — on-call, at scale, with real accountability.

What makes my profile stand out is deep financial domain expertise: at StockX I design subledger-to-GL pipelines, reconciliation frameworks, and audit-ready data flows that Accounting and Tax teams rely on for global month-end close. I speak the language of both engineers and finance stakeholders, which is rare.

I also integrate AI tooling — Claude Code, prompt engineering, LLM-driven workflows — into daily engineering practice. Not as a novelty, but as a genuine productivity multiplier that compresses delivery cycles without sacrificing quality.
